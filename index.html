At Hodgson Sports Massage Exeter, we work closely with the client to provide an individual service - we do not subscribe to a one sizes fit all approach to massage treatment. We are driven to ensure treatment is right for you and you are happy with our service!

Our Exeter Sports Massage service is a hands on treatment that mobilises soft tissue, which is very effective at maintaining the body and keeping niggling injuries at bay. Sports massage and deep tissue massage is not just for sports people, it can be a very effective way of keeping on top of the everyday stresses of life, for example back pain from working at a desk or from lots of driving, or aches pains as a result of a physical job. Fundamentally treatment can be a great way to help achieve your goal whether that be in a sports setting (eg treatment of a sports injury, pre event or post event massage and recoveryu for training) alturnatively massage therapy is a great way of keeping on top of occupational aches and pains from your everyday life.

Our Exeter massage treatment can be stand-alone or can be done alongside other treatment such as physio, chiropractor or osteopathy treatment, this can be very effective.

"Our commitment to you" - we are in the business of helping people! Therefore if we don't feel our service is best for you at the time, we will always look to refer you and make no charge.
