At Hodgson Sports Massage Exeter, we work closely with the client to provide an individual service - we do not subscribe to a one size fits all approach to massage treatment. We are driven to ensure treatment is right for you and you are happy with our service!

Our Exeter Sports Massage service is a hands-on treatment that focuses on mobilising soft tissue to help you feel better. Sports massage and deep tissue massage is not just for sports people, it can be a very effective way of keeping on top of the everyday stresses of life and the resulting aches and pains.

Treatment can be stand-alone or can be done alongside other treatment such as physio, chiropractor or osteopathy treatment.

"Our commitment to you" - we are in the business of helping people feel better! Therefore, if we don't feel our service is best for you at the time, we will always look to refer you and make no charge.
