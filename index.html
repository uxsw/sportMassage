At Hodgson Sports Massage, we work closely with the client to provide an individual service - we do not subscribe to a one sizes fit all approach to treatment. We are driven to ensure you are happy with our service!

Our Sports Massage service is a hands on treatment that mobilises soft tissue, which is very effective at maintaining the body and keeping niggling injuries at bay. Sports and deep tissue massage is not just for sports people! It can be a very effective way of keeping on top of the everyday stresses of life, for example back pain from working at a desk or from lots of driving, or aches pains as a result of a physical job. Fundamentally treatment can be a great way to help achieve your goal whether that be in a sports setting or in your everyday life!

Our massage treatment can be stand-alone or can be done alongside other treatment such as physiotherapy chiropractic or osteopathy treatment, this can be very effective.

Our commitment to you - we are in the business of helping you! Therefore if we don't feel our service is suitable for you we will always look to refer you and make no charge.
