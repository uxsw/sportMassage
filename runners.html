Sports Massage for Runners

At Hodgson Sports Massage, Exeter we have a bit of previous when it come to running, well nearly 50 years of experience including road, track, cross country and multi terrain racing with Mark and Clare combined to be precise!! We certainly don't profess to know everything but over the years we have learnt a lot and made a lot of mistakes (and paid the price with injury and illness!).

Through our passion for sport and in particular running we feel well placed to offer treatment to runners and also advise in other areas such as:

  • Training
  • Nutrition
  • Running Gait Anlysis
  • Running shoes/kit
  • Strength & conditioning

We geniunely do get a buzz from working with people just starting out on their running journey through to elite athletes chasing medals on the world stage
